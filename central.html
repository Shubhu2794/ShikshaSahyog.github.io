Eligibility Criteria

  • Students of SC/ ST/ BC/ Disabled/ Minority communities with an income ceiling of Rs.2.00 lakhs per annum.
  • For 5th to 10th Class students belongs to community SC/ ST/ Disabled are eligible
  • Students belong to BC community are only eligible for applying 9th and 10th.
  • He / She Should have an Aadhar Card, in case he /she does not have, he /she shall secure an Aadhar card by visiting the Enrollment center.
  • He/she should be a regular, full time student studying in Government School or in a School run by local bodies such as Mandal Praja Parishad, Zilla Praja Parishad, Municipalities/ Municipal Corporations or any school aided by the Government.
  • If a student has to repeat a class, he/she shall not get a scholarship for that class for a second (or subsequent) year.
  • Bank Account: Each Student shall have a Bank Account in his/her name from any of the Nationalized Banks, students who are minors shall have a joint account in the name of the student and mother/father/guardian.

Description

The AP Pre Matric Scholarship 2023 is a financial assistance program offered by the Government of Andhra Pradesh. The scholarship is designed to help students who belong to economically weaker sections of the society to continue their studies. The scholarship is available for students studying in classes 5 to 10.
