AP Government Scholarships


Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC, ST, BC and Disabled Welfare Students

Description: The AP Pre Matric Scholarship 2023 is a financial assistance program offered by the Government of Andhra Pradesh. The scholarship is designed to help students who belong to economically weaker sections of the society to continue their studies. The scholarship is available for students studying in classes 5 to 10.

Eligibility:

  1. Students of SC/ ST/ BC/ Disabled/ Minority communities with an income ceiling of Rs.2.00 lakhs per annum.
  2. For 5th to 10th Class students belongs to community SC/ ST/ Disabled are eligible
  3. Students belong to BC community are only eligible for applying 9th and 10th.
  4. He / She Should have an Aadhar Card, in case he /she does not have, he /she shall secure an Aadhar card by visiting the Enrollment center.
  5. He/she should be a regular, full time student studying in Government School or in a School run by local bodies such as Mandal Praja Parishad, Zilla Praja Parishad, Municipalities/ Municipal Corporations or any school aided by the Government.
  6. If a student has to repeat a class, he/she shall not get a scholarship for that class for a second (or subsequent) year.
  7. Bank Account: Each Student shall have a Bank Account in his/her name from any of the Nationalized Banks, students who are minors shall have a joint account in the name of the student and mother/father/guardian.

Scholarship Amount:

  1. Rs.100 per month for Boys for 10 months
  2. Rs.150 per month for girls for 10 months.

Post-Matric Scholarship for SC, ST, BC and Disabled Welfare Students

Description:The AP Post Matric Scholarship is a scheme initiated by the Government of Andhra Pradesh to provide financial assistance to students from economically weaker sections to pursue post-secondary education. The scholarship aims to encourage students to pursue higher education and to help them overcome financial barriers.
The disbursal of scholarship is categorized into two schemes;

  1. Reimbursement of Tuition Fee (RTF) / Tuition Fee Reimbursement.
  2. Maintenance Charges (MTF).

Eligibility:

  1. All the students, pursuing Polytechnic, ITI and Degree & above level courses in Government/ Aided/ Private colleges, affiliated to State Universities / Boards.
  2. Day scholar students, students in College Attached Hostels (CAH) and Department Attached Hostels (DAH).
  3. 75% of the aggregate attendance is mandatory for release of scholarships.
    Income Eligibility
  1. The total family annual income to be less than or equal to Rs. 2.50 lakhs.
  2. The total land holding of the family to be less than 10.00 acres of wet or 25.00 acres of dry or 25.00 acres both wet and dry land together.
  3. No member of the family should be a government employee/ pensioner (all sanitary workers irrespective of their salary/ recruitment, are eligible.
  4. The Social Welfare Department shall develop a robust & fool-proof system of certification of parents of the beneficiaries under category of “Sanitary workers”).
  5. No member of the family should own a four-wheeler (Taxies/ Tractors/ Autos are exempted).
  6. A family who owns no property or less than 1500 Square feet of built up area (Residential or Commercial) in urban areas is eligible.
  7. No member of the family should be an income tax payee.

Scholarship Amount:

  1. Reimbursement of Tuition Fee (RTF) / Tuition Fee Reimbursement.
    • Provide full fee reimbursement to every eligible student, i.e. Full Fee (Tuition Fee, Special Fees, Other Fees & Exam Fees) as defined in the G.O.Ms.No.66, SW(Edn) Dept., dated 8-9-2010 and as fixed by the competent authorities is reimbursed to all eligible students.
    • Full fee shall be credited to the respective College accounts on behalf of students.
  2. Maintenance Charges (MTF).
    • Rs.10,000/- per person to ITI students,
    • Rs.15,000/- per person to Polytechnic students,
    • Rs.20,000/- per person for other Degree and above courses per year to every eligible student for food and hostel expenses.

Application Link: Jnanabhumi Application

Veda Vyasa Scheme for Vedic Education (VV-VE)

Description:Veda Vyasa Scheme for Vedic Education (VV-VE), Andhra Pradesh invites applications from students who belong to Brahmin community of Andhra Pradesh and are pursuing full-time Vedic Education. This scholarship provides financial assistance to the selected students in order to encourage Vedic Education.

Eligibility:

  1. The applicant must fall in the age group of 8 to 19 years.
  2. The applicant and his/her parents must be from a Brahmin community and the parents must reside in Andhra Pradesh.
  3. The applicant must be pursuing a full-time Vedic course in any Private Veda Patasala registered with Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) or Endowments Department of Andhra Pradesh or enlisted by the A.P. Brahmin Welfare Corporation.
  4. If the student is receiving lodging and boarding expenses from TTD/Endowments Department, he/she is not eligible for this scholarship.

Scholarship Amount:

Bharati Scheme for Education (BSE)

Description:The purpose of AP Brahmin Bharti Yojana is only one, all Brahmin committees living in Andhra Pradesh should be given the best facilities for higher education to all students. This scheme has been offered to Brahmin students since many years ago.

Eligibility:

  1. Student's parents must be from the Brahmin community to obtain a scholarship.
  2. Also, the student's parents must reside in Andhra Pradesh.
  3. Students of class 1 to postgraduate are eligible to apply for this scholarship.
  4. Aspirants must be a permanent resident of the Andhra Pradesh state.
  5. Students belonging to the 13 districts of Andhra Pradesh can apply for this scholarship.
  6. The candidate must have passed all the examinations till the previous year of education.
  7. Once applied for this scholarship, the student cannot be avail of the benefits from any other scholarship scheme, except the benefits from Archaka Welfare Trusts.
  8. The student should be enrolled in Praja Sadhika Survekshan (PSS).
  9. The students family income (total income of all the family members) should not exceed three lakhs in a year.
  10. This scholarship scheme is awarded to those students who complete their studies through the regular medium and not in open or distance learning mode.
  11. Orphans, differently-abled students and children of a single mother are eligible for this scholarship, even if they are studying in private school.
  12. The candidates must score at least not less than 50% to avail of the scholarship.
  13. All students must bring at least 50% percent in their studies to obtain the scholarship.

Scholarship Amount:

  1. Selected students will get financial assistance of up to INR 20,000/-. The amount awarded to the scholars varies depending upon the level of study.

NTR Videshi Vidyadharana Scholarship Scheme for BC and EBC Students

Description:A lot of different types of overseas scholarships are available at the epass portal through which the candidates are eligible to get scholarship benefits even if they are studying abroad. These scholarships are a way to help candidates take professional educational courses not available in India. This scholarship is specially presented by the Backward Classes Welfare Department Government of Andhra Pradesh state. The financial assistance will only be provided to the students belonging to the backward class categories.

Eligibility:

  1. Backward Classes students; family income should not be more than Rs. Six Lacs (6,00,000) per annum from all sources to apply for NTR Videshi Vidyadharana Overseas Scholarship.
  2. The students must enclose the income certificate which should obtain through MEE SEVA. A copy of the latest Tax assessment, as well as the latest salary slip from the employer, is also needed to be submitted with the application.
  3. The BC candidates' age should not be more than 35 years as of 1 July 2019.
  4. For Post Graduate Courses: A degree in Engineering / Management / Pure Sciences / Agriculture Sciences / Medicine & Nursing / Social Sciences / Humanities is the educational criteria.
  5. For Ph.D. Course: PG course in Engineering / Management / Pure Sciences / Agriculture Sciences / Medicine / Social Sciences / Humanities is the educational criteria.
  6. PG courses include PG Diploma courses of One year, and One and a Half year duration.
  7. The scheme includes the study of CPA (Certified Public Accountant) and CPM (Certified Portfolio Manager) courses in the US since they are equivalent to Chartered Accountancy (CA) / Cost Accounting (CA) courses in India.
  8. The scheme is available for only one child of the same parents or guardian.
  9. A self-certification will require from the candidate in this regard.
  10. The scholars are not eligible to get this award again as it will give award to one student only once in a lifetime.

The scholarship will only be available in the following countries:-
USA, UK, Australia, Canada, Singapore, Germany, New Zealand, Sweden, Netherlands, France, Denmark, Russia, Philippines, Kazakhstan, and China (Philippines, Kazakhstan, and China only for medicine).

Scholarship Amount:

  1. Instalment-I: An amount of Rs.5.00 lakhs shall be payable to the students upon production of the landing permit /I- 94 card. (Immigration Card)
  2. Instalment-II: An amount of Rs.5.00 lakhs shall be payable to the students upon production of the 1st Semester results.

Chandranna Videshi Vidya Deevena Scheme

Description:Andhra Pradesh State Kapu Welfare and Development Corporation Limited (APSKWDCL), a Government of Andhra Pradesh undertaking, is introducing the “Chandranna Videshi Vidya Deevena Scheme (2016-17)” for the economically weak Kapu youth in Andhra Pradesh. Under this scheme, a financial assistance of Rs 1,000,000 (Rupees Ten Lakh Only) shall be granted to the selected applicants through the prescribed process. This assistance shall be for studying overseas in recognized universities for tuition and living expenses.

Eligibility:

  1. The applicant should belong to Kapu community i.e. the Kapu, Balija, Telaga and Ontari sub-castes of Andhra Pradesh.
  2. The annual income limit of the parents shall be Rs 600,000 (Rupees Six Lakh Only) or below.
  3. At the time of admission into the foreign institute, the applicant should have received his/her Intermediate certificate/relevant Graduate Degree for applying for MBBS courses or Graduate/PG certificate for applying for PG/Ph.D. courses.
  4. PG courses include PG Diploma courses of One year, and One and Half year duration.
  5. The scheme includes the study of CPA (Certified Public Accountant) and CPM (Certified Portfolio Manager) courses in the United States of America, since they are equivalent to Chartered Accountancy (CA) / Cost Accounting (CA) courses in India.
  6. The applicant should be 35 years of age or below while applying.
  7. Any Individual can avail this scheme only once in a life, and cannot be considered for the second or subsequent times.
  8. Studying in courses from recognized universities from USA, UK, Australia, Canada, Singapore, Germany, New Zealand, Sweden, Netherlands, France, Denmark, Russia, Philippines, Kazakhstan and China (Philippines, Kazakhstan and China for medicine only) shall be considered for this scheme.
  9. Application to other countries can be considered from case to case basis by the State Level Selection Committee.
  10. In the case where not all scholarships are availed by eligible candidates, students already studying in 1st year or going to join 2nd year in eligible foreign institutes and courses, as above, may apply for this scheme.
  11. The applicant should not have availed himself/herself of any amount under any other Government scheme for the same purpose.

Scholarship Amount:

  1. Instalment I -An amount of Rs 500,000 (Rupees Five Lakh Only) shall be payable to the students upon production of the landing permit / I-94 card. (Immigration Card).
  2. Instalment II -An amount of Rs 500,000 (Rupees Five Lakh Only) shall be payable to the students upon production of the 1st Semester/Term results.

NTR Vidyonnathi Scheme

Description:NTR Vidyonnathi is the State Level Scheme. In the year 2015, the Andhra Pradesh State Government has introduced this Scheme for giving high Professional Guidance for Civil Service Exams. The Main Objective of this Scheme is to provide Free Civil Service Coaching to the Reputed Institutes in the Country.

Eligibility:

  1. Age: The Selection Committee will accept the application forms based on their age limit. The minimum age limit is 21 years and the maximum age limit is 37 years. The Disabled Candidate’s upper age limit is 42 years.
  2. Income Ceiling: For candidates who are going to apply for this program, their annual income should not exceed Rs. 6 lakhs.
  3. Selection Process: Applied Candidates are going to Select through the Entrance Exam. Candidates who will get higher marks in the written Test, will be eligible for this Free Coaching Program. And the reputed IAS Academy Institutes will send the Allotment Order to the Selected Applicants.
  4. Application Fee: There is no Application fee for this program. Beneficiaries can apply online without paying any amount.
  5. Qualification: The Required Education Qualification is Any Degree. And must complete their Bachelor’s Degree from any reputed University or State & Central Govt Universities. The Final Year Students are not eligible to apply for this Scheme.

Combined Counselling Board (CCB) Scholarship

Description:Combined Counselling Board (CCB) Scholarship 2023 is an opportunity offered by the Combined Council Board of India to students who are studying from Class 0 to postgraduation. The Welfare Department of CCB provides financial support and scholarship to students in collaboration with Government approved educational institutions, NGOs and Trusts for obtaining higher education. The selected candidates will receive up to 100% scholarship on their selected courses.

Eligibility:

  1. The applicant should have a degree based on the course which he/she opts for.
  2. The candidate should have scored at least 40-50% marks in the last qualifying examination.
  3. The candidate pursuing Polytechnic should have scored at least 33% marks in Class 10 or Class 12 examination.
  4. The applicant pursuing BBA/BCA and Engineering in Mining, Aeronautical, Petroleum, and Fire & safety should have scored a minimum of 40% in the last qualifying examination.
  5. The candidates pursuing BTech Lateral Entry, BArch, BTech in Agriculture, Biotech, and BDS should have scored at least 50% marks in the last qualifying examination.

Scholarship Amount:

  1. Selected candidates will be reimbursed up to 80% of their tuition fees (INR 60,000- INR 2,50,000 depending on the course).
  2. The scholars are also provided with a hostel fee concession
